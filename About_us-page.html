About us

Inter Energy was founded in 2016. We are located in Hefei city, which is very close to Shanghai. Our company covers an area of 5000 square meters and has 150 employees. Inter Energy - national high-tech enterprise specializing in research and development, production of solar modules, construction, operation and maintenance of solar power stations.

Our products have been approved by international certification such as TUV, CE, CEC, INMETRO, FIDE, etc. As well as domestic certification such as CQC, Leader, ISO9001, ISO14001, OHSAS18001, etc.

Inter Energy has already taken root in the domestic market, and at the same time we are actively developing the international market and have established a sales network in more than 30 countries. Inter Energy has its own research and development team of 15 specialists, and we are constantly investing in product research and development and have already obtained more than 10 patents.

By focusing exclusively on the development and production of solar polycrystalline and monocrystalline modules, we have been able to consistently improve and develop new products to better serve our customers. We keep up with new industry standards and introduce new technologies. Thanks to a strong development team, Inter Energy produces photovoltaic modules of excellent quality and value. The global strategy of Inter Energy is to achieve leadership in a complex market and contribute to the conservation of the planet's resources.
